Discover Powerful Insights

Reflection's app leaderboard ranks all apps in the store according to the money they make or the number of times they are downloaded.

Our Story

Revenue, download and rank data is an essential source of product and commercial insight that until now was only available to the few members of the development community who could afford to pay the hefty fee for it.

Reflection is revolutionising the app industry by providing freemium access to a comprehensive intelligence and analytics platform comprised of a set of tools our partners and members have helped to create. These include some of the app industries biggest names such as Peter Molyneux OBE, Toca Boca, Sega, Miniclip, Soundcloud etc etc etc

The accuracy of our data is all-important and maintained through detailed mathematical algorithms developed in conjunction with our statistical partner, Imperial College London.
