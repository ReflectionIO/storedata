Our Mission

Reflection Exists to Help Developers Make More Successful Apps

We do this by providing them with the market information they need to make better decisions

Our Story

Accurate app market intelligence is important to everyone who makes apps. It allows you to track the performance of other apps & developers and identify what type of apps and business models achieve the best results.

Affordable app market intelligence is hard to come by and it takes time to analyse this data to make the right decisions.

Reflection was founded by app developers who saw an opportunity to make accurate app market intelligence available for free. We aim to build premium features and tools that help developers understand the app market better, saving them time, effort and resources.

Our goal is to build the only market intelligence platform app developers will ever need
