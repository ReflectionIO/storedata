A Bit About Us

All app developers would benefit from reliable market intelligence but the barriers to it are high. Reflection is the first intelligence provider that is built by developers, for developers with free access to app market data and affordable reporting tools that help make more informed business decisions.

Unlike App Annie, Think Gaming, Priori Data and Sensor Tower, Reflection is not only affordable but has a delightful, well designed user experience, transparency on costs and data accuracy as well as a super simple automated sign up process.
